We price our carpet in two ways:

- "Per Square Foot" is our unit price which measures the area of a flat surface, e.g., 3 ft. long x 3 ft. wide = 9 sq. ft.

- "Per Linear Foot" measures the length of a single roll of carpet, e.g., a linear foot of a 10 ft. wide roll of carpet = 1 ft. x 10 ft. = 10 sq. ft.
